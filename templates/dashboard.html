Manual Correction (Add External Registrations)

This is used for adding real registrations from other channels (offline, ads, website etc).
Bot will display: real_count + manual_add
Example: 50 (adds +50 to Today count)
Example: 2000 (adds +2000 to Month count)
⚠️ Suggestion: Month Add should be larger than Today Add to look normal.
